Core Values About Our Programs

The design of Messy Woodwork, Nature Play & Wall-less Classroom can cater children with different developmental stages, in which they can take part in creative, child-led and open-ended play opportunities with appropriate setup.

Children flourish when given ample time and space to freely play in a sensory-rich and engaging nature environment. With these elements, MESSY MAKER helps them grow into healthy, balanced and resilient young people.

Children can use their endless creativity to design their own way to play. With our proper risk assessment in the environment, facilitators are always listen to the ideas of children and step back to let them try, in which nurture independence and cultivate curiosity.

Children are the future stewards of the Earth. They must develop a deep love of it in order to passionate about protecting the environment when become an adult.

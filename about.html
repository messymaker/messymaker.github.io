play workers About Messy Maker

Messy Maker is passionate about true messy play opportunities. We carry open-ended, child-led and nature-play deep in our hearts and have experience in creating engaging programmes for children to play in and learn in

We carefully assess risks and benefits and listen to the children, step back and facilitate real play. We see ourselves as play worker

Apart from creating and designing new wood projects, programmes and nature experiences, we also work in partnership with parents and other educators in promoting child-led play by sahring our own experiences. Our role is hard to define but we surely do a lot of multi-tasking!
